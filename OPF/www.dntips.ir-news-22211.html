Why Go is Going Nowhere


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۶ ۲۱:۴۳
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
Go, a popular board game, is struggling to gain global traction due to disagreements among Asian nations regarding its rules. China’s withdrawal from a Seoul tournament and Japan’s potential sale of its headquarters highlight the challenges. The game’s commercial appeal is fading, and younger generations are increasingly choosing chess, shogi, and video games.
