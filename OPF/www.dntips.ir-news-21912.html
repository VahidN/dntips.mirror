31 Days of Vibe Coding – Day 13: Stop AI From Changing What You Didn't Ask For


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۳ ۱۶:۲۸
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This article discusses the increasing tendency of AI to “improve” code, leading to unsolicited changes and potential issues. It highlights the importance of explicitly defining scope and using the “Ask First” pattern to control the impact of AI-driven modifications. The article also introduces the “Show Don’t Apply” pattern for risky changes and emphasizes the need for careful review and approval before implementing any changes.
