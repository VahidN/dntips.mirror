Washington State Microchip Ban Sparks Debate on Worker Privacy


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۴ ۱۷:۱۲

A bill proposing a ban on employers requiring microchipping of employees is gaining traction in Washington state, sparking concerns about worker privacy and bodily autonomy. The bill, introduced by House Bill 2303, would prohibit employers from using such technology for workplace management or surveillance.
