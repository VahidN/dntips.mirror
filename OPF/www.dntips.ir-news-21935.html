Grammar Girl Podcast – Using ‘impact’ as a verb. –


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۳ ۱۶:۴۳
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
The Grammar Girl podcast recently explored the evolving use of “impact” as a verb, moving beyond its traditional meaning of force to encompass a broader sense of influence and effect. Irving, the podcast host, highlighted how “impact” is increasingly used to describe a positive influence, rather than a negative one. The discussion also delved into the linguistic shift between “sympathy” and “empathy,” with the podcast arguing that “sympathy” began to sound patronizing and “empathy” expanded to encompass a more nuanced understanding of shared experience. The episode also touched on the podcast’s Patreon rewards system, offering a range of levels for listeners.
