Filtering as domain logic


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۳۰ ۱۲:۰۷
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This article explores the strategic use of server-side filtering within applications, particularly in database-driven systems. It examines how to leverage client-side libraries to manage complex filtering logic, addressing concerns about duplication and ensuring maintainability. The article highlights the benefits of separating filtering logic from the core application code, utilizing techniques like test-driven development and property-based testing. It also discusses the role of SQL and Cypher query languages, and the potential for client-side filtering to handle more intricate selections.
