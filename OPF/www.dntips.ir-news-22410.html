Trump’s China Strategy: Global Survey Reveals Growing Confidence in Chinese Influence


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۹ ۱۸:۵۶
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
A recent global survey reveals a significant shift in the perception of China’s global influence, with majorities in nearly every surveyed territory anticipating its continued growth. The survey, conducted by the European Council on Foreign Relations (ECFR), indicates a decline in US perceived reliability among traditional adversaries and an increase in support for rearmament among European allies, particularly in response to Russia’s growing influence. The survey also highlights a growing distrust of the US among some European nations, with a notable increase in support for China as a rival.
