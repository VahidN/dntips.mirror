Grammar Girl Podcast – Penny idioms that are still legal tender. The linguistic history of procrastination. Tanner tour.


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۱۸ ۱۲:۲۷
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This week’s episode of Grammar Girl Quick and Dirty Tips for Better Writing explores the fascinating history of penny idioms – from "a bad penny" to "penny wise and pound foolish." We delve into the linguistic roots of procrastination, examining how words like "soon," "anon," and "presently" evolved from meaning "at once" to "in a little while." Karen Lunde, a long-time writer, shares insights into the evolution of these phrases and their enduring relevance. We also discuss the Tanner tour, a fascinating look at the evolution of web design and marketing.
