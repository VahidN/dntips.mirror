Qwen3-TTS Family – A New Era of Voice Generation


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۱/۰۳ ۲۰:۱۷
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This report details the release of Qwen3-TTS Family, a suite of advanced multilingual, controllable, robust, and streaming text-to-speech models. The family includes tokenizers and models, available under the Apache 2.0 license, facilitating community research and development. Extensive testing demonstrates state-of-the-art performance across diverse benchmarks.
