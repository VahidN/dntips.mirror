New Book: Vibe Coding Video Games with Python


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۳۰ ۱۶:۲۲
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
Mike’s latest book, “Vibe Coding Video Games with Python,” explores the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and game development. The book delves into creating mini-games using AI, focusing on replicating the visual style and gameplay of classic video games. It’s a practical guide for aspiring game developers, offering insights into leveraging LLMs like Google Gemini, Grok, and Mistral to build these unique experiences. The book covers various game types, from simple clones to more complex scenarios, and emphasizes the limitations and potential of AI in this creative field. It’s a blend of technical instruction and thoughtful exploration of the possibilities.
