AI-Augmented Workflow: Less Cognitive Effort, Enhanced Collaboration


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۷ ۰۳:۱۹
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This article explores how AI tools are transforming software development workflows, shifting the focus from extensive requirements gathering to iterative refinement and leveraging AI for drafting and code assistance. It highlights the benefits of AI in automating initial tasks, suggesting a shift towards human-AI collaboration where AI assists in the upfront work, reducing the investment in materials and fostering more dynamic feedback loops.
