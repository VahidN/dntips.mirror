This episode explores the evolving role of Developer Relations (DevRel) in the software engineering landscape. Fred Harper, a veteran developer, shares his 20+ year journey, discussing the shift from traditional developer roles to a more collaborative, community-focused approach. We delve into the challenges and rewards of being a DevRel, examining the importance of empathy, communication, and building genuine relationships with developers. Discover how to determine if DevRel is the right path for you, and learn practical strategies for success.
