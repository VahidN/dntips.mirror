Beyond Hallucinations: The Illusion of Understanding


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۱۸ ۱۹:۱۹
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
Mike Amundsen’s paper explores the concept of epistemic drift – the gradual slide into an illusion where LLMs confidently produce outputs mistaken for factual truth – and proposes a framework to mitigate this through three key traps: Map vs. Territory, Intuition vs. Reason, and Conflict vs. Confirmation. The research highlights the importance of recognizing LLM outputs as probabilistic echoes rather than definitive statements, fostering a more cautious and human-centered approach to LLM usage.
