Grammar Girl Podcast - Rare Books, Burned Letters, and Johnson’s Dictionary


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۱۸ ۱۸:۳۶
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This week on the Grammar Girl Quick and Dirty Tips podcast, we delve into the fascinating life and legacy of Samuel Johnson, the renowned lexicographer. We interview John Overholt, curator at Harvard’s Houghton Library, about Johnson’s eclectic career, the challenges of managing a collection of 4,000 rare books, and the enduring appeal of seemingly unremarkable items. We also explore the process of digitizing and preserving these invaluable historical documents.
