AI’s Impact on Jobs: A Collaborative Future


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۱۸ ۱۶:۱۶
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This podcast episode explores the evolving role of Artificial Intelligence in the workforce, shifting from a potential job killer to a powerful collaborator. Pete Johnson, Field CTO at MongoDB, discusses how AI embeddings and vectorization are driving productivity gains and offers practical insights for realizing ROI. The discussion also features insights from Werner Vogel’s keynote and explores the challenges and opportunities presented by AI-driven automation.
