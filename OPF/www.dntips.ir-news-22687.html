Microsoft Security – Phishing and BEC Campaign Targeting SharePoint


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۱/۰۲ ۱۶:۵۳
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
A recent multi-stage AI-powered phishing and BEC campaign is exploiting vulnerabilities in SharePoint, leveraging Microsoft Sentinel and Defender for Cloud to target organizations across various industries. The campaign utilizes sophisticated techniques to impersonate legitimate users and gain access to sensitive data. Microsoft Security is actively investigating and responding to the threat.
