Google Deepmind’s podcast episode discusses the development of Sec-Gemini, a security-focused Large Language Model (LLM), and its capabilities compared to general-purpose LLMs. The episode explores the motivations behind building Sec-Gemini, its strengths, and how it differentiates itself in the security landscape.
