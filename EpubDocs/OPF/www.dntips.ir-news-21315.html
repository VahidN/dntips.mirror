Man’s Search for Meaning


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۱۸ ۱۲:۵۲
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
Frankl’s “Man’s Search for Meaning” explores the enduring human capacity to find purpose even in the face of unimaginable suffering. The book details his experiences in Nazi concentration camps and his development of logotherapy, a therapeutic approach centered on finding meaning in life. Frankl argues that meaning is not inherent but must be actively created through choosing one’s attitude toward unavoidable suffering. He emphasizes the importance of “the attitude of the hero” – a courageous and purposeful response to adversity. The book highlights the power of human will and the potential for resilience, suggesting that even in the darkest of times, individuals can find a reason to live.
