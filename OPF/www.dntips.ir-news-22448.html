This article discusses a critical issue in event-driven read model rebuilding, highlighting the dangers of relying on a single, fixed rollback strategy. It explores the "Rabbit Hole of Fixes" and the challenges of handling asynchronous events, ultimately advocating for a more robust approach involving Dead-Letter Queues and careful monitoring.
