Hack Reveals a16z-Backed Phone Farm Flooding TikTok With AI Influencers


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۱۸ ۱۹:۱۰
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
A hacker gained control of a 1,100 mobile phone farm powering covert, AI-generated ads on TikTok. Doublespeed, a startup backed by Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), has been hacked, revealing the products the AI-generated accounts are promoting without disclosure. The hack exposes the company’s vulnerabilities and allows the hacker to take control of more than 1,000 smartphones.
