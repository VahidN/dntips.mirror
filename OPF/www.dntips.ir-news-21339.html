wa-tunnel: Tunneling Internet traffic over Whatsapp


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۱۸ ۱۳:۱۵
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
wa-tunnel is a Baileys-based piece of code that allows TCP data to be tunnelled through two Whatsapp accounts. It’s designed for network carriers and airplanes with unlimited WhatsApp data or social network data, utilizing Baileys for multi-user support.
