Syntax Podcast Episode #971: Stackoverflow and Firefox are Dead?

تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۳۰ ۱۶:۳۷
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This episode explores the evolving landscape of web development, focusing on the decline of Stack Overflow, the impact of AI on software development, and the changes occurring with Firefox and Apple’s browser engine. Wes Bos and Scott Tolinski discuss the implications for developers, highlighting new tools, documentation, and browser updates. They also delve into the challenges of AI-driven development and the importance of continuous learning.
