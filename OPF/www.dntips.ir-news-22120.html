Context, Tokens, and When to Compact


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۵ ۱۶:۲۹
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
This article discusses the challenges of maintaining context in large AI conversations, particularly when dealing with lengthy development sessions. It explains how AI models degrade context over time, leading to inconsistencies and the need for intervention. The article highlights key indicators of context degradation and provides a practical command, the "compact" command, to mitigate these issues.
