Wikimedia Foundation Announces New AI Partnerships with Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Perplexity and Others


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۷ ۰۶:۲۲
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
The Wikimedia Foundation announced a series of new partnerships with AI tech companies, including Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Mistral AI, and Perplexity, as part of its 25th birthday celebration. These partnerships will allow Wikipedia to leverage AI for large-scale reuse and distribution of content, providing access to Wikimedia projects to tech companies. The foundation also launched a birthday campaign, including a behind-the-scenes look at volunteers and a “25 Years of Wikipedia” time capsule.
