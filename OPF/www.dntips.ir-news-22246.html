InfoQ Podcast: Why Engineering Culture Is Everything: Building Teams That Actually Work

InfoQ Software Architects' Newsletter: A monthly overview of things you need to know as an architect or aspiring architect. This episode explores the critical role of engineering culture in building successful teams, focusing on the importance of trust, collaboration, and psychological safety. Vivek Yadav discusses how Stripe’s approach to testing, leveraging Apache Spark, and the shift towards Spec-Driven Development exemplifies this. The podcast also touches on the challenges of scaling testing and the benefits of data-driven architecture.
