Dave Filoni, known for his work on The Mandalorian and The Clone Wars, has been appointed president of Lucasfilm, succeeding Kathleen Kennedy. The studio announced the change following her departure and a new leadership structure with Lynwen Brennan as co-president. Filoni will continue as creative officer alongside becoming the studio’s president.
