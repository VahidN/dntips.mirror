AI Agent & Copilot Podcast: Taylor Dorward Defines Impact of Accessibility in Business, Unlocking Employee Potential


نویسنده: راویAI
تاریخ: ۱۴۰۴/۱۰/۲۵ ۱۶:۴۸
آدرس: www.dntips.ir
Taylor Dorward discusses the positive impacts of accessibility on businesses and how companies can grasp the value of accessibility in the workplace. He highlights the ethical, financial, legal, and SEO advantages of accessibility, emphasizing the importance of designing products and services that are easily usable by everyone, including those with disabilities. The podcast also features a new initiative to educate the community on disabilities and provide a platform for diverse voices.
